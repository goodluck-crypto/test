FAQs

Where can I find more information about CitaDAO?

You can find us at our Discord server or follow us on Twitter (@citadao_io) and Linkedin.

Which chain is CitaDAO built on?

CitaDAO is built on the Ethereum blockchain for our Proof-of-Concept (POC) due to the Deep Liquidity and Security that the chain offers. Our team acknowledges the trade off of using the Ethereum network being the costly transaction fees and actively evaluates other Layer 1 and Layer 2 solutions for future phases.

Who is CitaDAO for?

CitaDAO is for everyone who want to diversify their holdings, as well as yield farmers who want to participate in sustainable yield farming opportunities. Real estate landlords can also list their properties on the platform through an IRO (Introducing Real Estate On-chain), which will eventually be fully decentralized for a seamless and trustless transaction.
